6. How to open the link target in the same page without disturbing the existing content?
A. By using <iframe> 
    - <iframe> allows to embed the contents of any
      another page into the current page, without
      disturbing the contents on page.
    - <iframe> introduced into HTML from version HTML5.
    - Before <iframe> we were using 
           <frame> and <frameset>

  Syntax:
    <iframe  src="page.html" width="100" height="100" noborder  name="refName"> </iframe>
